About the artist

All of our artwork is done by 16 year old Rachel Harris. She set up Photraits because she wanted to make high quality, affordable art available to everyone. With Photraits, anyone can have their own personalized, unique piece of art. Rachel draws realistic people and pets in watercolour or pencil.
